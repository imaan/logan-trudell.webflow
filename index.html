A little about me

For the past 5 years, I've been acting as a sales lead for a global talent network that consists of top designers, developers, marketers, product managers and consultants.

Selling such a wide variety of solutions requires a bullet-proof system for ensuring you can always make the perfect pitch on any given day, and that is the system I work with my clients to implement.

I have an unwavering passion for understanding exactly why people make the decision to purchase, and I use this knowledge to ensure that every single email, phone call, landing page and pitch deck your customers engage with work together to tell an engaging story that positions you as the only option.
